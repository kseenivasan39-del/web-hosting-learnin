01

Project "Pustakalaya"

Education & Literacy • 2024

Recognizing the lack of quality reading resources in remote parts of our district, I led a youth campaign that mobilized donations to establish 3 community-run libraries.

Impact Accomplished: Open access to standard competitive exam preparation guides, non-fiction and textbooks for over 1,200 rural students.
02

Heritage Defense Collective

Historical Preservation • 2024-2025

To prevent encroachment and neglect of valuable 18th-century local heritage pillars, we mobilized community cleaning drives and petitioned the local administration for active archaeological survey protection.

Impact Accomplished: Successful registration of the local monument with State Archeology department; placed directional informational boards detailing local oral history.
03

RTI Awareness Workshops

Democracy & Right to Information • 2025

Collaborating with constitutional law students, we organized workshops for rural self-help groups (SHGs) on filing Right to Information (RTI) petitions. This empowered local women to seek transparent metrics on MGNREGA disbursements.

Impact Accomplished: 40+ local citizens successfully learned how to draft RTIs; uncovered key distribution backlogs in ration cards.
04

Right to Education Access Drive

Education Policy Outreach • 2025-2026

We initiated a doorstep enrollment program mapping underprivileged children to local schools under Section 12(1)(c) of the RTE Act. This section mandates a 25% reservation for weaker sections.

Impact Accomplished: Supported 85+ children from marginal farming families in obtaining proper school admissions and materials.
